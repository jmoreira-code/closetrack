CloseTrack
Month-end close management, backed by SharePoint
No SharePoint yet? Click "Use Demo Mode" to explore with sample data. Connect SharePoint when you're ready to go live.
1
Create three SharePoint Lists
In your SharePoint site, go to New → List and create three lists named exactly:
CT_JournalEntries   CT_Reconciliations   CT_Checklist

For each list, add the columns shown in the setup guide below.
2
Enter your SharePoint site URL
Paste the URL of your SharePoint site (e.g. https://yourcompany.sharepoint.com/sites/Finance)
Found in your browser bar when viewing your SharePoint site.
3
Enter your close period
What month/year are you closing?
SharePoint List Setup Guide
CT_JournalEntries — add these columns (Single line of text unless noted):
  • Description
  • Account
  • Amount (Number)
  • Preparer
  • Reviewer
  • DueDate
  • Status (Choice: Open, In Prep, In Review, Complete, Overdue)
  • Period
CT_Reconciliations — add these columns:
  • Account
  • GLBalance (Number)
  • SubBalance (Number)
  • Preparer
  • Reviewer
  • Status (Choice: Open, In Prep, In Review, Complete, Overdue)
  • SignedPreparer (Yes/No)
  • SignedReviewer (Yes/No)
  • Period
CT_Checklist — add these columns:
  • Task
  • Category
  • Owner
  • DueDate
  • Status (Choice: Open, In Prep, In Review, Complete, Overdue)
  • Period
Tip: The "Title" column already exists in every SharePoint list — you can rename it or leave it. CloseTrack uses the columns listed above.
